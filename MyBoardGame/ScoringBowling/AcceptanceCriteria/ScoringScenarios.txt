Given I have a Game In Progress
And I am on my first scoring frame
And Its my attempt 1
When I knock down 4 pieces
The score of 4 is recorded by the board

Given I have a Game In Progress
And I am on my first scoring frame
And Its my attempt 1
And I already knocked down 4 pieces
When I knock down 3 pieces
The score of 7 is recorded by the board

Given I have a Game In Progress
And I am on my second scoring frame
And Its my attempt 1
And I already scored 7
When I knock down 10 pieces (a strike)
